Angular Expertise

As a Software Engineer with over 16 years of experience in the IT industry, I have developed a deep expertise in Angular. My journey with Angular began with its second version and has continued through its latest updates, allowing me to stay at the forefront of this powerful framework.

My experience with Angular includes creating dynamic, responsive web applications that provide a seamless user experience. I have a strong understanding of Angular's core concepts such as components, services, directives, and modules. This knowledge enables me to architect applications that are modular, maintainable, and scalable.

In addition to backend development, I have also worked with frontend technologies such as JavaScript, AJAX, JQuery, and Angular 2.0+. This experience enables me to create interactive and user-friendly interfaces, enhancing the overall user experience.

In addition to the core framework, I have also worked extensively with related libraries and tools such as RxJS for reactive programming and NgRx for state management. These tools have allowed me to build applications that are robust and can handle complex, asynchronous operations with ease.

My proficiency in TypeScript further enhances my Angular development skills, enabling me to write safer, more reliable code. I also have experience with HTML5, CSS3, and pre-processors like SASS, which I use to create visually appealing and accessible interfaces.

Throughout my career, I have adopted best practices in coding, testing, and deployment. I am well-versed in unit testing with Jasmine and Karma, and end-to-end testing with Protractor. I also have experience with CI/CD pipelines, ensuring that the applications I develop are continuously integrated and deployed.

This portfolio showcases some of the Angular projects I have worked on. I invite you to explore these projects to get a sense of my capabilities as an Angular developer. I look forward to opportunities where I can leverage my Angular expertise to create impactful web applications.
