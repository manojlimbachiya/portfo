.NET Expertise

With over 16 years of experience in the IT industry, I have honed my skills as a seasoned Software Engineer specializing in .NET technologies. My proficiency extends to database design and development on MS SQL Server, where I have hands-on experience in indexing, creating tables, functions, complex stored procedures, and views. This expertise allows me to create robust and efficient database systems that can handle large amounts of data and complex queries.

My work with WCF has further equipped me with the skills to develop and consume web services, facilitating seamless data exchange between different software systems.

Throughout my career, I have consistently strived for excellence, adopting best practices in coding and software design. I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends in .NET and other relevant technologies. This commitment to growth and innovation is reflected in the projects I have undertaken, some of which are showcased in this portfolio.

I invite you to explore my work and look forward to opportunities where I can leverage my .NET expertise to create impactful software solutions.
